# Privacy Policy for French Vocab Drill **Last updated: July 16, 2026** French Vocab Drill respects your privacy. This policy explains what information is handled when you use the app. ## Information We Collect French Vocab Drill does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or other personal information. Your vocabulary progress and app settings are stored locally on your device. This information is not uploaded to our servers. ## Purchases French Vocab Drill offers an optional one-time in-app purchase to unlock all modules. Payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment card details. We use RevenueCat to verify purchases, restore purchases, and determine whether the full version has been unlocked. RevenueCat may process purchase-related information, such as: * Purchase history * Product purchased * Purchase status * An anonymous app user identifier * Basic device and app information needed to provide the service This information is used only to manage in-app purchases and access to paid content. You can review RevenueCat’s privacy policy on the RevenueCat website. ## Analytics and Advertising French Vocab Drill does not use third-party advertising. The app does not use your information for targeted advertising or track you across apps and websites. The current version does not use third-party analytics services. ## Data Sharing We do not sell your personal information. Purchase-related information may be processed by Apple and RevenueCat as necessary to complete, verify, and restore purchases. ## Children’s Privacy French Vocab Drill does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not require an account or ask users to submit personal information, we do not knowingly maintain personal profiles of children. ## Data Retention and Deletion Vocabulary progress and settings stored locally can be deleted by removing the app from your device. Purchase records may continue to be retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their own legal and operational requirements. ## Changes to This Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s features or data practices change.
