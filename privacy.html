Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

French Vocab Drill respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected, how it is used, and your choices when using the app.

Information We Collect

French Vocab Drill does not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or other personal information.

Your learning progress and app settings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your study data.

In-App Purchases

French Vocab Drill offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock all learning modules.

Payments are securely processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

  • An anonymous app user identifier
  • Purchase status
  • Product purchased
  • Basic device and app information necessary to provide purchase services

Analytics and Advertising

French Vocab Drill does not display advertisements.

The current version does not use third-party analytics services and does not track you across apps or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information.

Purchase-related information is processed only as necessary by Apple to provide in-app purchase functionality.

Children's Privacy

French Vocab Drill does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
